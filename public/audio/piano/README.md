Piano samples
=============

Source: University of Iowa Electronic Music Studios Musical Instrument Samples.

Dataset page: https://theremin.music.uiowa.edu/mispiano.html
License/use page: https://theremin.music.uiowa.edu/mis.html

Original files used:

- Natural notes: Piano.mf.C2.aiff through Piano.mf.C6.aiff.
- Black keys: Piano.mf.Db2/Eb2/Gb2/Ab2/Bb2 through Piano.mf.Db5/Eb5/Gb5/Ab5/Bb5.

The AIFF files were converted to WAV and trimmed with a short fade-out for web playback.
Sharp spellings in the app reuse the matching flat-named sample file.
